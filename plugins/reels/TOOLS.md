## Reels Plugin

AI-powered Instagram Reel yaratuvchi. Mavzu bering — tayyor video oling.

### create_reel
Mavzu asosida to'liq Instagram Reel yaratadi:
- GPT-5.4 bilan ssenariy yozadi
- ElevenLabs bilan voiceover (sizning ovoz kloningiz)
- Pexels dan kontentga mos b-roll footage
- Word-by-word animated captionlar
- Mood-based background musiqa
- SFX (whoosh, bass, ding)
- 1080x1920 vertical MP4

**Misol:** `create_reel({"topic": "QR-kod 2026 dan majburiy"})` → tayyor .mp4 fayl
